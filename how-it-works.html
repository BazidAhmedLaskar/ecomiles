How EcoMiles Works

EcoMiles gamifies sustainable transportation to reduce pollution and reward eco-friendly choices

🚨 The Problem We're Solving

🏭

Air Pollution

Transportation accounts for 24% of energy-related CO₂ emissions globally

⛽

Fuel Consumption

Private vehicles consume vast amounts of fossil fuels daily

🚗

Traffic Congestion

Overcrowded roads lead to longer commute times and increased emissions

💸

Economic Cost

High fuel costs and maintenance expenses for private vehicle owners

💡 Our Solution: Gamified Sustainable Transportation

🎯

Incentivize Good Choices

Reward users with points for choosing eco-friendly transportation

📱

Easy Tracking

Simple manual entry or GPS tracking for journey logging

🏆

Competitive Element

Leaderboards and badges encourage friendly competition

💰

Real Benefits

Convert points to tangible rewards like road tax deductions

🌱 EcoPoints Scoring System

🚶

Walking

10 points/km

Highest reward for zero emissions

🚴

Cycling

8 points/km

Great for longer distances

🚌

Public Transport

5 points/km

Includes bus, train, metro

🚘

Carpool

3 points/km

Sharing reduces emissions

Additional Benefits

CO₂ Calculation:
1 km = 0.21 kg CO₂ saved
Road Tax Formula:
₹1 per 10 EcoPoints
